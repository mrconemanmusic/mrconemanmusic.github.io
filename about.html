About

Hi — I’m Ciaran “MrConeman” Lyttle, a Northern Ireland–based composer for games & media. I deliver game-ready assets: seamless loop edits, stems for interactive scoring, and mixes that leave room for SFX and voice. I’m currently taking on indie projects and can provide a short custom demo to see if we’re a fit.

I’ve had a lifelong relationship with games and music—they grew up together for me. I studied music at school, became a multi-instrumentalist (guitar/bass/keys/programming), and in recent years I’ve focused on writing and producing original cues with modern tools (Cubase + high-quality virtual instruments) alongside live guitars where it serves the track.

Outside of game scoring, I release music as MrConeman (including the lofi/boom-bap album There’s A Lot Going On on Spotify) and I’m the guitarist/songwriter in Dead Addiction (…of Vice and Men). I also take on session/freelance work, which keeps my production chops sharp and my workflow fast. If you’re looking for someone collaborative, responsive, and comfortable delivering stems, alternates, and loop-ready mixes, I’d love to chat.
