About

Hi — I’m Ciaran “MrConeman” Lyttle, a composer for games & media, based in Belfast, Ireland. I deliver game-ready assets: seamless loop edits, stems for interactive scoring, and mixes that leave room for SFX and voice. I’m currently taking on indie projects and can provide a short custom demo to see if we’re a fit.

I’ve had a lifelong relationship with games and music — they grew up together for me. I studied music at school, became a multi-instrumentalist (guitar/bass/keys/programming), and in recent years I’ve focused on writing and producing original cues with modern tools (Cubase + high-quality virtual instruments) alongside live guitars where it serves the track.
